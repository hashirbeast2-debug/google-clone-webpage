# Blackjack Game 🃏

A browser-based Blackjack game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## How to Play
1. Click **START GAME** to deal your first two cards
2. Click **NEW CARD** to draw another card
3. Try to reach 21 without going over
4. Click **NEW GAME** once the round is over to play again
